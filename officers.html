The Officers of GADIG

President

Eric Kosinski
Contact

Favorite Game: Tales of Abyss

Eric Kosinski has been in GADIG for over 3 years. A huge card game fan, Eric spends much of his time researching competative card games and is always up for a match. You can also challenge Eric to a game of Smash Bros during any fun friday. Eric is also activley involved with George Mason's Game Design Department, helping with open houses. If you ever have any questions about the club you can always go to Eric.

Vice President

Matt Kelly
Contact

Matthew Kelly has been in GADIG for over a year. A junior in Computer Science, you will often find Matt assisting the programming team while helping lead the club. While not at GADIG meetings Matt likes to play a large variety of games in order to examine their merits from a design standpoint.
